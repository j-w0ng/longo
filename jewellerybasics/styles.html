Ring Styles

Home > Engagement Ring Basics > Ring Styles

Bezel Setting

A thin piece of metal wraps the rim of the diamond, creating the most secure method of setting the diamond.

Cathedral Setting

Ring setting using arches of metal to hold the diamond in place.This is the most classic style of ring.

Halo Setting

Encompassing the largest center diamond with smaller diamonds making the central diamond look even bigger.

Solitaire Setting

The simplist of ring settings, the diamond is held up by prongs, showing off the diamond and creating the most sparkle.

Tension Setting

Nothing but he pressure of the band holds the diamond in place, no prongs, bezel or other mountings.
